Here you can visualize an individual player's performance for different statistics:

Statistic Meaning
% This is a player's scoring percentage. If a player scored 5 out of 10 attempts, this will yield a socring percentage of 50%.
%_goalie This is a goalie's saving percentage. If a goalie saved 3 out of 10 shots, this will yield a saving percentage of 30%.
2' A player's suspension count.
7M Penalty scoring percentage.
7M_goalie Goalie: penalty saving percentage.
D Disqualifications (Red Cards)
P-W_goalie not properly implemented yet. Will show absolute saves / absolute attempts on goal for goalies.
TF Technical Fouls (Double Dribble, Steps...)
TORE not properly implemented yet. Will show absolute goals / absolute attempts for outfield players.
V Yellow Cards


Start by selecting a Team, a Season and a Statistic you are interested in. You will then be able to select individual players to compare against each other.

Hover over a line in the graph to see which player it belongs to.
